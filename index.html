The Don Killuminati: The 7 Day Theory

Album by Tupac Shakur

The Don Killuminati: The 7 Day Theory is the fifth studio album and first posthumous album by American rapper Tupac Shakur, credited as the alias Makaveli. It was released on November 5, 1996, almost two months after his death, and was released through Death Row Records, Makaveli Records and Interscope Records.
